Catch Green Bob!

Try to return the stolen rewards.

Catch Green Bob is a fun reaction game where Green Bob keeps jumping around the screen every time you try to click him. Green Bob has stolen your rewards! Click on him as many times as you can before the time runs out to get your rewards back! Be quick, be precise, and keep chasing him with your mouse until you finally manage to catch him and reclaim your stolen points!
